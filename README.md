# Food-Safety-Recall-Analysis
An analysis of USDA-FSIS food recall data to identify patterns, repeat offenders, and seasonal trends.
## Project Overview
This project analyzes publicly available data from the USDA-FSIS to investigate patterns in food recalls, including common recall reasons, repeat offenders, and seasonal trends. This analysis was conducted as part of my Google Data Analytics portfolio.

## Key Questions Answered
1.  **What are the most common reasons for food recalls?**
2.  **Which companies are repeat offenders and what are their recall patterns?**
3.  **Do recalls happen more frequently during certain times of the year?**

## Key Findings & Insights
- **Product Contamination** is the leading cause of recalls.
- **Tyson Foods, Inc.** is the top repeat offender.
- The **Summer** season, particularly the months of **June and July**, has the highest frequency of recalls.
